## Day 1 — College Park to Burlington

Straight shot up I-95 then cut across to Vermont. The moment you cross into Vermont the highway disappears and the trees take over.

## Day 2 — Burlington to Waitsfield

{% include trips/map-section.html id="day2" stops=page.day2_stops type="road" %}

Burlington farmers market in the morning, then wound south through Stowe and down into the Mad River Valley. Peak foliage was about a week out but the colors were already going.

## Mad River Glen

Famously co-op owned, no snowboards allowed. In September it's just a quiet gondola ride up to an empty summit.
